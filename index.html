FAQ

Common Questions

Do you do acrylics?

No. Little Canvas exclusively uses gel products. This includes gel polish, builder gel (hard gel), and Gel-X soft gel extensions. No acrylics, no dip powder applications.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online through Vagaro at vagaro.com/littlecanvasnailstudio. Select your service, choose a time, and you are all set. Please book at least 24 hours in advance.

What should I expect for my first visit?

Please arrive with bare nails (no existing product). When you get to Phenix Salon Suites, the front door will be locked. Text Page at (804) 481-5141 and she will meet you at the door.

How much does nail art cost?

Nail art starts at $15 for French tips and simple accents. Hand-painted designs range from $20 for basic art up to $60 or more for intricate full-set designs. Chrome is $5. 3D sculpted art starts at $10. Nail art pricing is in addition to your base manicure service.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged a fee of 50% of your scheduled service price.

Do you offer nail repairs?

Yes. Nail repairs within 10 days of your last appointment are free. After 10 days, repairs start at $10.

Is the studio private?

Yes. Little Canvas is a private suite inside Phenix Salon Suites. Your appointment is one-on-one with Page in a clean, comfortable space. No crowded salon, no waiting.
